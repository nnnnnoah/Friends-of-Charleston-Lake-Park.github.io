Frontenac Arch Biosphere Maps

The Frontenac Arch Biosphere Reserve, of which CLPP is a crown jewel, offers a wealth of information. For a comprehensive listing and guides to area trails; walking, hiking, paddling, cycling and snowmobiling, visit Frontenac Arch Biosphere.

Are you interested in trips in Eastern Ontario?

We are very proud of our area and would love to tell you about it. Besides the official Ontario Road Map, the map of Eastern Ontario is an excellent map of the whole area from the Quebec border to Oshawa and is well done. This map has all the federal, provincial, county and township roads identified, complete with names. All lakes, rivers are also clearly identified.
