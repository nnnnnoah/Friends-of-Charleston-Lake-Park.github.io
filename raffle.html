Annual Raffle

Win a 16' H20 Canoe

The Friends raise funds to help out with improvements at the Park, fund our regular activities and support special events. the Annual Raffle is part of that effort. Besides this great canoe as first prize, the second prize is a season pass valid at any Ontario Provincial Park.

Tickets for each raffle cost $5.00 each or $10.00 for 3.

They will be available at the Park Gatehouse, canoe rentals, and local
venues including Ribfest and Craft Fair in Gananoque, the Athens Corn Fest and the
Lyndhurst Turkey Fair.


Here are the 2022 winners of our raffle!

1st prize, H20 Prospector Canoe

David Kraitberg, Brockville, ON

2nd prize, 2023 Ontario Parks Season Pass

Thanks to all who bought raffle tickets.
All proceeds go to the Park, in support of its programs and activities.
