Notice of Annual General Meeting

The Friends AGM will be held on Saturday, April 26, at 10 a.m. at the Park's Main Office.

Agenda:

  1. Receipt of the Agenda
  2. Receipt of the Minutes of the 2023 AGM
  3. Approval of the 2024 Financial Report (followed by a resolution “to waive an independent audit
    or financial review'” [This is a requirement of the Ontario Non-Profit Corporations Act])
  4. Proposed amendment of the Friends' by-laws (“that Article VIII.2 “Committees shall be chaired
    by Directors-at-large” be amended to read “Committees shall be chaired by Directors.”)
  5. Report on the Pavilion project
  6. Park Report
  7. Election of Directors
  8. Adjournment
